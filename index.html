My Story

My name is Manuel Martinez, I am 20 years old, and this is my story. I was born in 2005 in the town of Calarcá, Quindío. At the age of 4, I moved to Bogotá, where I grew up and attended several schools throughout my academic journey. As I shared earlier, my path hasn't been a straight line; it has been a journey of exploration—from emergency services to the world of technology—driven by a desire to learn and help others.
